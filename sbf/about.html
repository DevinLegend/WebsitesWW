Our Story

No Job
Too Tough

For over half a century, South Bay Fence, Inc. stands as a testament to family commitment and unparalleled craftsmanship in the fencing industry. Anchored by our motto, "No Job Too Tough," we offer expertise in various fencing solutions, from chain link fence to iron fence, specialized cages, water valve covers, and safety netting. Every fence we construct is backed by our unwavering dedication to quality. A simple call to us will address your inquiries and guide you towards the perfect fencing solution.

Having been an integral part of Chula Vista for over five decades, our work is likely familiar to you, even if our name isn't. Professionals across industries – from contractors and architects to realtors and landscapers – count on us. South Bay Fence values these long-standing relationships. But don't just take our word for it; reach out and let us demonstrate our excellence firsthand.

We pride ourselves on our ability to tackle diverse projects. Serving countless homes and businesses throughout San Diego yearly, our reputation speaks volumes of the quality and reliability we offer.
